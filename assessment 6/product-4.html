History Of the LAND ROVER

Land Rover is a British brand of predominantly four-wheel drive , off-road capable vehicles , owned by multinational cars manufacturer Jaguar Land Rover (JLR),since 2008 a subsidiary of India's Tata motors .JLR currently builds Land Rover in Brazil , China, India ,Slovakia , and the United Kingdom.The land Rover name was created in 1948 by the Rover Company for a utilitarian 4WD off-roader; yet today Land Rover vehicles comprise solely upmarket and luxury sport utility cars.[14]
